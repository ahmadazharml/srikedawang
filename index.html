SRI KEDAWANG Travel & Tours (w) Sdn Bhd was incorporated in 30th November 1998 and becoming a leading travel operator in Malaysia is our main objective. As a travel agency, we are registered with the Ministry of Finance (Reg. No. KKKP1609). After 12 years of establishment we are proud to say that we are now a full fledged accredited travel agency.We are a member of the Malaysian Association of Tour and Travel Agents (MATTA) .

Being a full fledged agency, we cover every aspect of traveling necessities such as worldwide flight bookings (be it Inbound or Outbound flights in any country) and hotel reservations. All services related to your travel arrangements can be conducted by us. We operate with integrity and exceptional expertise to ensure our clients feel respected and special through surpassing and top-notch services.Our Mission is to perform and deliver excellent quality services to our clients. With this objective, we aim to earn the reputation as "Your Preferred Travel Agency".

This is in line with travel industry to cater the nation's thrust towards globalization by the year 2020. Since the tourism industry has been a major contributor towards our economic growth in recent years, government efforts and incentive will commensurate a better environment to the industry operators.Through the conspicuous leadership of our founder, the dedication of our managing team, the commitment of our staff and the trust of out clients, we have elevated ourselves to a prominent level of success and development. We are confident that we will flourish to an even greater level and become a key player in the thriving tourism industry in Malaysia and in the Southeast Asian region.
